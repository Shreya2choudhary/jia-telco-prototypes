Use case 3 — Call Intelligence

Jia answers the calls you can't

When a call comes in and you don't pick up, Jia steps in on your behalf — takes the message, surfaces it as a live transcript, and lets you listen in, join, or decline at any time.

3A

Known callerYou don't answer in 5 rings — Jia auto-answers and takes the message.

Scenario: Ananya (saved contact) calls. The phone rings 5 times — your default ring count, configurable. You don't pick up, so Jia auto-answers, asks why she's calling, takes the message about a birthday dinner, and pushes a summary card you can act on.
3A.1 · Call + Messages

Messages notification

Mid-call, Jia drops a Messages banner with a live transcript. Tap it to see Jia and Ananya's bubbles streaming in real time, with three chips: Listen in, Join the call, or Decline.

3B

Unknown callerPhone never rings — Jia intercepts on the cloud and screens the call.

Scenario: An unknown number calls — Rohit from HDFC Bank pitching a pre-approved credit card. Phone never rings; Jia intercepts at the network and answers directly. A notification slides down silently with a live transcript. The user reads two exchanges, recognises it as a sales call, taps Decline. Jia closes politely, summary card flags the number as spam.
3B.1 · Screen + Messages

Messages notification

Notification banner appears at the top of the lock screen. Tap it to open the live Messages transcript — Jia and Rohit's bubbles streaming, with three chips: Listen in, Take the call, or Decline.

3C

Add Jia to a callPull Jia onto an in-progress call. Three triggers — notification (live), wake word (next), short code (next).

Scenario: You're on a call with Ananya planning dinner tonight. The weather looks iffy. Mid-call, a notification drops at the top: "Add agent to call". You tap it → an entry tone plays, a system voice announces "AI assistant has joined the call. Jia." → Jia is now on the line as the third participant, listening silently. You ask her a question (the weather), she answers, then goes back to listening. You and Ananya finish planning, lock in 7:30 at Bastian, hang up.
3C.1 · Notification

Tap to add Jia

Mid-call, a notification banner drops at the top of the screen. One tap brings Jia in as a third party — entry tone, system announcement, then she listens until you ask her something.
